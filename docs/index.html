Kontu

App prototyping drag and drop tool with user-siloed data

We are building an infrastructure for anyone (technical or non-technical) to be able to create working prototype apps by combining different modules/services in a plug & play fashion.

In addition to that, Kontu follows a user-siloed data approach:

  1. Data is on the user's device.
  2. User decides what modules/services have access to their data and in what order.
  3. A functional prototype app is manifested.

When we are done, investors won't ever read a pitch without an accompanying working prototype :)
